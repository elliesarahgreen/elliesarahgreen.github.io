I led on the development and implementation of a research and article series, the 'Hiring Trends Index'. These insights were then compiled into articles featuring infographics, to provide employers with a timeley snapshot of the UK market, often before the Office of National Statistics published their own. This positioned the Totaljobs brand as a thought leader and expert within the market, able to frequently provide in-depth insigths and analysis.
